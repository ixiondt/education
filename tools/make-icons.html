Generate PNG icons

Android & Chrome PWAs work fine with the SVG icons that ship in ../icons/. iOS Safari prefers PNG for the home-screen icon. Click the button below to render the SVG at the sizes iOS & Android Play Store expect, then save each PNG into the icons/ folder next to index.html.

The manifest already references these filenames: icons/icon-192.png, icons/icon-512.png, icons/icon-maskable-512.png, and (for iOS) icons/apple-touch-icon.png.
